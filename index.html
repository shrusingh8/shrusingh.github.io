The Pitfalls of Multiple-Choice Questions in Generative AI and Medical Education

Journal: Scientific Reports

Read Article

LLM performance on medical benchmarks may be inflated and illusory, driven by factors beyond medical knowledge and reasoning. To assess the validity of this hypothesis, we created a medical benchmark of free-response questions and recorded the performance of SOTA LLMs. The results presented an average absolute deterioration of 39.43%. Masking studies further solidified this claim as LLMs performed at 37.34% accuracy at 100% masking!
